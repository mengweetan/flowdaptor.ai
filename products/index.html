Flagship · Proprietary

FlowDaptor 1.0

A proprietary AI e-commerce engine that automates multi-platform selling for Singapore SMEs. Unlike rigid rule-based tools or single-purpose AI, we deploy a society of specialised AI agents — Listing, Pricing, Inventory, Customer, Asset, Settlement — that coordinate through a shared semantic memory, enabling compound intelligence that learns, adapts, and improves.

The problem we solve

Singapore SMEs selling across Lazada, Shopee, Shopify, and TikTok Shop face:

  • 15–20 hours/week manual listing & pricing sync per 100 SKUs
  • 8–12% error rate in pricing mismatches and stock discrepancies
  • 15–25% revenue loss from stockouts and reactive pricing lagging 24–48h
  • SGD 500–2,000/month on creative production with zero personalisation
  • 4–12 weeks to settle property and vehicle transactions, with 3–8% fees

Large retailers use AI. SMEs are stuck with spreadsheets and manual work.

The society of agents

Listing

Auto-generates optimised listings per platform. Learns what converts.

Pricing

Dynamic optimisation balancing competitors, margins, inventory, and demand elasticity.

Inventory

Predictive restocking. Forecasts 14–30 days ahead and prevents stockouts.

Customer

Cross-platform identity resolution. Segments audiences and tailors promotions.

Asset

AI-generates images, videos, and banners personalised per platform and segment.

Settlement

Tokenised title and smart-contract atomic settlement for real assets — under 24h, under 1% cost.

Coordination

Resolves conflicts between agents. Optimises for system-wide outcomes, not local maxima.

All agents share a unified semantic memory — product ontologies, market state, customer profiles, action history. The pricing agent knows inventory constraints; the customer agent knows pricing elasticity.
