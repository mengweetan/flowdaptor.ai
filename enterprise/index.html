FAQ

Questions enterprise procurement actually asks.

Do you sign NDAs and pass our vendor onboarding?

Yes. We complete vendor questionnaires, sign mutual NDAs, and participate in security reviews. Most of our enterprise clients put us through their standard vendor onboarding before signing.

Can we run this fully air-gapped?

Yes. We've deployed into environments with no outbound internet access, using on-prem LLMs and internal-only data sources. Trade-offs are real (some integrations require connectivity), and we'll be specific about them during scoping.

What happens if we want to leave?

The workflows are yours. n8n is open-source. We hand over a runbook, source-of-truth repository, and an offboarding session. No lock-in by design — that's the whole point.

Do you support 24/7 operations?

Our standard SLA covers business hours. 24/7 on-call is available as an add-on, with response targets tuned to your specific workflow criticality.

Can you work with our existing automation team?

Often the best outcome. We design with handover in mind — your team co-owns the build, learns the patterns, and inherits a system they actually understand.
