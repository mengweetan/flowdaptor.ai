FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The course is designed for operators and analysts. n8n is visual-first — you'll write zero traditional code. We do cover basic JavaScript expressions on Day 2, but nothing beyond a spreadsheet formula in complexity.

What do I bring on Day 1?

A laptop. We provide the n8n environment, sample datasets, LLM credits for in-class use, and a real workflow to take home and adapt.

Is there a certificate at the end?

Yes. Successful completion includes a WSQ Statement of Attainment plus a FlowDaptor practitioner certificate confirming you've shipped a working agent.

How does the subsidy actually work?

You pay the gross fee. We submit grant documentation. Approved subsidies are reimbursed directly to your SkillsFuture / SFEC account or processed against the invoice depending on the funding pathway.
