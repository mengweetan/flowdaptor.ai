FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The course is designed for operators and analysts. n8n is visual-first — you'll write zero traditional code. We do cover basic JavaScript expressions on Day 2, but nothing beyond a spreadsheet formula in complexity.

What do I bring on Day 1?

A laptop. We provide the n8n environment, sample datasets, LLM credits for in-class use, and a real workflow to take home and adapt.

When is the next cohort?

Workshops and cohorts are scheduled on demand. Drop us a line at our contact page with your team size and target dates and we'll come back with options.

Can you run this privately for our team?

Yes — most of our training is delivered in-house. We tailor the syllabus to your stack and use your real workflows as the final-project case study.
